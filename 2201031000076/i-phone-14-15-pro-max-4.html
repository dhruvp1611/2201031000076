The lion (Panthera leo) is a large 

cat  of the genus  Panthera ,

native to Africa and India.

It has a muscular, broad-chested

body; a short, rounded head;

round ears; and a hairy tuft at

the end of its tail. It is  sexually

dimorphic ; adult male lions

are larger than females and

have a prominent mane. It is a 

social species , forming groups

called prides.
