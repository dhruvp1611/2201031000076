TIGER

The tiger was first  scientifically

described  in 1758. It once ranged

widely from the  Eastern Anatolia

Region  in the west to the  Amur

River  basin in the east, and in the

south from the foothills of the

  Himalayas  to Bali in the  Sunda

Islands . Since the early 20th

century, tiger populations have

lost at least 93% of their historic

range
